💡 System Tips & Settings

🚀 Short Tutorial
  1. Create Columns: Click + Add Column to organize your workflow (e.g., "Review", "Deployment").
  2. Add Tasks: Type a title in the input box at the bottom of a column and hit Enter.
  3. Organize: Simply drag and drop task cards to move them between different stages.
  4. Manage Tags: Head to the Tags section in the sidebar to create custom colored labels.
  5. Archive: Once a task is in "Done", use Clear Done to move it to your Archive for safe keeping.
💡 WIP Limit
The Working On column has a Work In Progress (WIP) limit.
Maximum allowed: 3 tasks.
If you exceed this, the column header will turn red to alert you of potential bottlenecks.
⌨️ Keyboard & Usage Tips
  • • Drag and Drop: Move cards between columns to change status.
  • • Task Deletion: Click the small [x] on a card.
  • • Column Management: Use [rename] to change titles or [remove] for empty columns.
  • • Automatic Saving: Your data is saved to localStorage instantly.
  • • Enter Key: Quickly save tasks, columns, or tags by pressing Enter.
